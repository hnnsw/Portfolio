For a couple of years I've been working intensively on UI/UX design. As standards change constantly in our world , I think that creating an interface between design and technology is indispensably important. I am always hungry to keep growing, learn new things, exploring the world and experiencing new adventures and challenges.

You have a challenge for me or just want to talk about design-related topics? Feel free to say hello.

How did I become a UI/UX Designer?

Back in school, I implemented a large web project with a school colleague as a thesis. It quickly became clear to me how important user experience and interaction design are. We would have saved a lot of time if we had known more about this topic back then. Since then I have started to deal with UI/UX design more intensively. It soon became clear to me that my passion was in this area because I love that I can improve the world a little bit, just with my creativity. I had the chance with a bunch of others to design and implement a new software as a service solution for decentralized and predictive energy management to reduce costs and maintenance in buildings.

You want to get detailed information about my work? Check out my projects.

What's my mission?

As a creative, I want to take advantage of the infinite boundaries of design and make as many users as possible happy. I'm always open to work with like-minded people and learn from the best to improve my own knowledge.
